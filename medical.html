What We Do

Comprehensive equipment care for practices that can't afford downtime or failed inspections.

⚡

Electrical Safety Testing

Leakage current, ground continuity, and insulation testing performed to recognized standards such as NFPA 99, helping keep patient- and staff-facing equipment safe.

🛠️

Equipment Repairs

Fast diagnosis and repair of malfunctioning devices — from electrical and mechanical faults to worn components — to get your equipment back in service quickly.

🗓️

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled inspections and performance checks that catch small issues before they become costly failures, extending the life of your equipment.

🎯

Calibration & Verification

Performance verification and calibration checks so your devices read accurately and operate within their intended specifications.

📋

Compliance Documentation

Detailed service records, test reports, and maintenance logs to support accreditation, audits, and inspection readiness for your practice.

📞

On-Call & Emergency Service

Responsive support when something goes down unexpectedly, minimizing disruption to your patients, clients, and daily operations.
